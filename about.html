About Us

We Offer Value

Columba Properties & Investment Limited. is a highly professional outfit with interest in real estate, Construction and project management effectively run by seasoned and highly motivated professionals. The decision of setting up the firm was borne out of a strong commitment to excellent service delivery, and by having to live with these challenges; the firm is committed to strict adherence to code and high ethical standards of practice.

Today, every business finds itself in a very competitive position, requiring huge investments to position well in its market, and real estate transactions involve one of the biggest financial investments most companies or individuals make in their lifetime. Columba Properties & Investment Limited has carved a niche in the real estate industry to take the hassles of property search and management off busy executives like you and to present you with balanced and reliable facts regarding property values, to enable you reach a beneficial decision. The company has been providing services to banks, oil companies, government institutions and other blue chip companies and would be delighted to serve you with greater commitment to excellence service delivery.
